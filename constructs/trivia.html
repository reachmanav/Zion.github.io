TRIVIA

Group multiplayer MCQ — Groq writes, Zion referees

In a WhatsApp group, Neo kicks off @zion trivia. Zion asks Groq for a batch of five multiple-choice questions, posts them one at a time with a 30-second clock, scores every A/B/C/D answer per player, and prints a final podium. Scores persist in trivia.db for bragging rights.
